## ü§î FAQ

### Q: I'm a consultant working for a government agency. Which license applies?

**A:** If you're contracted by a government agency *for government work*, you can use the free license. If you're a for-profit consulting firm offering D.A.V.E as a service to multiple clients, you need a Commercial License.

### Q: Can I use the free version to evaluate D.A.V.E before buying a Commercial License?

**A:** Yes! For-profit companies can evaluate D.A.V.E free for up to 30 days. After that, you must either stop using it or obtain a Commercial License.

### Q: What if I start as a non-profit and later become for-profit?

**A:** You must obtain a Commercial License before engaging in for-profit activities using D.A.V.E.

### Q: Can I contribute code improvements under the free license?

**A:** Yes! Contributions are welcome from everyone. By contributing, you agree your code may be used under both license types. See [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md).

### Q: What about SaaS companies offering compliance tools?

**A:** If you're a SaaS company using D.A.V.E as part of your service, you need a Commercial License. We offer revenue-share models for qualifying partners.

### Q: Can open-source projects use D.A.V.E?

**A:** Yes, open-source projects can use the free license as long as they're not part of a commercial offering.

### Q: What happens if I use D.A.V.E commercially without a license?

**A:** Unauthorized commercial use violates copyright law and the license terms. We actively monitor for compliance and will pursue legal action for violations, including damages and attorney fees.
